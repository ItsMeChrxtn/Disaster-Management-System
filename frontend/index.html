Preparedness

Simple actions before leaving home.

Use the map together with official barangay and municipal instructions. Keep plans practical, familiar, and easy to repeat.

1Check your barangay and the nearest visible hazard layer.
2Identify at least two nearby safe zones or evacuation centers.
3Prepare medicine, IDs, phone power, water, and essential supplies.
4Follow official advisories before floodwater, wind, or road conditions worsen.

FAQs

Common questions

What can I see on the public map?

You can inspect mapped hazards, safe zones, and evacuation centers for Cavite.

Does the map show my exact location?

Only when you click "Use my location" and your browser allows location access. The page uses it to center the map.

Can I search for a barangay or address?

Yes. The search box supports Cavite locations and uses OpenStreetMap Nominatim results.

Should this replace official emergency instructions?

No. Treat this as a public awareness tool and still follow instructions from local authorities and emergency responders.
